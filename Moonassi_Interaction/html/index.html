Moonassi

"I draw lines to forget about myself. I paint to end intangible thoughts,
hard feelings, and ghostlike delusions in my mind.
Lingering thoughts will be calmed by visualizing as it's tangled.
Raging feelings from the memories of the past will be cooled by otherizing them
with interacting bodies.
And my restless mind will be eased while I draw lines and paint black.
That is the reason I draw,
drawing scattering mind towards the past and the future,
not to forget about myself."
  • Daehyun Kim
